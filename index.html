uos : United Open-libraries of Sound..

uos unifies the best open-source audio libraries.

For fpc, fpGUI, MSEgui, Lazarus and Delphi.

With uos you can:

. Listen to mp3, ogg, wav, flac, aac, opus, cda, ... audio files.

. Deal with integer 16/32 and float 32 bit resolution.

. Do internet audio streaming of mp3 and opus files.

. Record all types of input into file.

. Add DSP effects and filters, however many you want and record it.

. Listen to multiple inputs and outputs.

. Create sound with the built-in synthesizer.

uos can use the SoundTouch, PortAudio, SndFile, Mpg123, AAC and Opus audio libraries.

Included in the package:

. Examples fpGUI, MSEgui, LCL(Lazarus) and console.

. Binary libraries for Linux 32/64, Raspi, Windows 32/64, Mac OSX 32 and FreeBSD 32/64.
