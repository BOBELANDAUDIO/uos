uos : United Open-libraries of Sound. uos unifies the best open-source audio libraries.

For fpc, fpGUI, Lazarus and Delphi.

With uos you can:

. Listen to mp3, ogg, wav, flac,... audio files.

. Deal with 16, 32 or float 32 bit resolution.

. Do internet audio streaming.

. Record all types of input into file.

. Add DSP effects and filters, however many you want and record it.

. Listen to multiple inputs and outputs.

uos uses the SoundTouch, PortAudio, SndFile and Mpg123 audio libraries.

Included in the package: . Examples fpGUI, LCL(Lazarus) and console. . Binary libraries for Linux 32/64, Windows 32/64, Mac OSX 32 and FreeBSD 32/64.
